About Me
Ever since a young age, I have refused to understand marketing as a concept that represents the mere amalgamation of fancy terms and grandiloquent jargons. In a world where essentially society progresses, effectively, we're all in a constant, albeit sustainable race: that of convincing someone, somebody out there, that what we have is better. I absolutely find it spectacular how people seem to fail to notice the strong literal connection between the terms 'market', and 'marketing', which are now used in entirely different connotations, yet share the same root word - and marketing, by principle, as The New York Times puts its best, is "the art of telling stories so enthralling that people lose track of their wallets.”
